# What Changed - Summary

## 5 Updates to Your RMA System

### 1. Customer Date Field
- Optional "Date Customer Opened RMA" field on RMA creation form
- Tracks when customer opened their RMA vs when you entered it

### 2. Weekly Notification Schedule  
- Check boxes for days (Sunday - Saturday)
- Time picker (like setting an alarm)
- Example: "Notify me Mon/Wed/Fri at 9:00 AM"

### 3. Dual Time Tracking
- Shows TWO times side-by-side:
  - Blue box: "In Our System" (how long you've had it)
  - Purple box: "Customer RMA Age" (how long customer has had it)
- Appears on dashboard and RMA detail page

### 4. New RMA Button
- â• "New RMA" button on dashboard header
- No more navigating to tabs to create RMAs

### 5. Cleaner RMA List
- Better organized layout on dashboard
- Time tracking boxes are subtle and clean
- Not overwhelming or too big
